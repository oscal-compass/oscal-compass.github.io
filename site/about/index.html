    About OSCAL Compass

    What is OSCAL?

    OSCAL is an opinionated approach to security management. The OSCAL Compass project is be comprised of an ensemble of tools that provide concise and uniform management of system security.

    The adoption unifies the approach to collecting, maintaining, and proceessing documentation artifacts for compliance needs. The projects enable end-to-end automation of compliance procedures. The and conducting proper scans of systems. The schema for OSCAL will bind the transferrability and provide a unified approach in support of multiple programming languages.
